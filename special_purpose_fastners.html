Special Purpose Nuts

Kohan Fasteners is the leading high-quality custom fasteners manufacturer and exporter in India. Industrial nuts are fasteners with threaded holes that, when paired with bolts or other parts, secure items together. Kohan Fasteners is a custom manufacturer and exporter with years of experience producing custom nuts in various materials and styles with unique features. We offer a variety of Industrial nuts in ready stock for export, crafted from materials such as Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, Aluminum, Titanium, and Inconel. Our custom nuts serve industries including Automotive, Lawn & Garden, Electronics, Industrial, and more.


We export various custom fasteners with diverse plating options, including different colors of Zinc, Black Oxide, Black Anodizing, Tin, Silver, Waxing, Passivation, Nickel, Antique Brass, Chrome, Cadmium, GM Spec, Magni Coating, Chrysler Spec, and more. We manufacture Industrial Nuts in both standard and non-standard specifications, tailored to meet specific customer requirements.


Our Special Purpose Nuts includes the following:

  • Coupling Nuts
  • Flange Nut
  • Hex Nuts
  • Hex Slotted Nuts
  • Heavy Hex Nuts
  • Lock Nuts
  • Square Nuts
  • Thumb Nuts or Knurled Nuts
  • T- Nuts
