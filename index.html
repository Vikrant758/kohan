About Us

Kohan Industries Pvt Ltd has been a leading fastener manufacturer since 1978, accumulating extensive experience over the decades. Our company is recognized for its expertise in producing a wide range of fasteners for various industrial applications. We specialize in manufacturing standard steel fasteners known for their durability, reliability, and high quality. Our long-standing industry presence has allowed us to refine our processes and continuously innovate, ensuring we deliver superior products that meet our clients' stringent demands. Committed to excellence and customer satisfaction, Kohan Industries Pvt Ltd is a trusted name in fastener manufacturing.
