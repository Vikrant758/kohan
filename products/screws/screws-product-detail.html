Allen Cap Bolts

An Allen cap bolt, also known as a socket head cap screw, is a type of fastener characterized by its cylindrical head and hexagonal drive hole. The term "Allen" comes from the Allen Manufacturing Company, which originally produced hexagonal socket screws and tools.

J Shoulder Bolts

A J shoulder bolt, also known as a shoulder screw, features an unthreaded shoulder between the head and the threaded part. The shoulder allows for precise alignment and smooth movement in mechanical assemblies. Commonly used in machinery, fixtures, and jigs, these bolts are made from materials like stainless steel and brass. They provide high strength and precision but may be more expensive and require specific tools for installation.


Technical Specification:

  • Threads: METRIC, BSW, BSF, UNC, UNF or as required
  • Standards: DIN, ISO, JIS, GB, IS, BS, ASTM and all International Standards.
  • Tolerance: +/-0.01mm to +/-0.05mm
  • Material: Alloy Steel, Exotic, Low Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel or as per required.Coating: Grey phosphate, yellow zinc plated, blue zinc plated, and others as per required.
