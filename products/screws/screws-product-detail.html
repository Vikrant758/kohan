Hex coach screws are heavy-duty fasteners designed for securing large or heavy materials. Featuring a hexagonal head, they provide a strong grip and are ideal for use in wood and masonry applications. Their robust design ensures reliable performance under high load conditions, making them suitable for structural and construction projects. Hex coach screws come in various sizes and materials to accommodate different needs and ensure durability.

Self Drilling Screws

Self Drilling Screws are designed for fast and efficient installation, eliminating the need for pre-drilling. These screws have a drill-shaped point that allows them to cut through materials like metal and wood, providing a secure and reliable fastening solution. They are ideal for applications requiring quick and strong assembly, making them perfect for construction and manufacturing projects.
