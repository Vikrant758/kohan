Bolts

Allen Cap Bolts

An Allen cap bolt, also known as a socket head cap screw, is a type of fastener characterized by its cylindrical head and hexagonal drive hole. The term "Allen" comes from the Allen Manufacturing Company, which originally produced hexagonal socket screws and tools.

Special Bolts

An Allen cap bolt, also known as a socket head cap screw, is a type of fastener characterized by its cylindrical head and hexagonal drive hole. The term "Allen" comes from the Allen Manufacturing Company, which originally produced hexagonal socket screws and tools.

J Shoulder Bolts

A J shoulder bolt, also known as a shoulder screw, features an unthreaded shoulder between the head and the threaded part. The shoulder allows for precise alignment and smooth movement in mechanical assemblies. Commonly used in machinery, fixtures, and jigs, these bolts are made from materials like stainless steel and brass. They provide high strength and precision but may be more expensive and require specific tools for installation.


Technical Specification:

  • J Bolts Diameter: M3- M60
  • Standard: ISO, DIN, DIN, ASTM, ANSI, JIS, EN
  • Threads: UNC, UNF, ISO, BSW & ACME
  • Coatings: Black Oxide, Cadmium, Galvanized, Teflon, Xylan, Zinc or other upon request.

U Hex Bolts

A U-bolt is a U-shaped fastener with threaded ends, used to secure pipes, tubes, or cylindrical objects. Commonly made from steel, they are used in piping, vehicle suspensions, construction, and marine applications. U-bolts provide a strong hold and are easy to install.


Technical Specification:

  • Size: M6 to M20
  • Threads: UNC, UNF, ISO, BSW & ACME
  • Imperial Size: 3/8 to 8”
  • Material: Alloy Steel, Exotic, Low Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel or as per required.
  • Standards: BS, DIN, ISO, ASME, UNI, DIN-EN and all international standards.

Flange Bolts

A flange bolt has a built-in washer (flange) under the head that distributes the load. It's commonly used in automotive and industrial applications for securing parts and reducing the need for separate washers.


Technical Specification:

  • Forming: Hot & Cold Forged
  • Size: M5 to M30
  • Imperial Size: 3/8 to 8”
  • Threads: UNC, UNF, ISO, BSW & ACME
  • Material: Alloy Steel, Exotic, Low Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel or as per requirement

Hanger Bolts

A hanger bolt is a fastener with a wood screw thread on one end and a machine screw thread on the other. It's used to join wood to metal or other materials and often employed in furniture assembly and mounting applications.


Technical Specification:

  • Imperial Size: 3/8 to 8”
  • Forming: Hot & Cold Forged
  • Threads: UNC, UNF, ISO, BSW & ACME
  • Standards: ASME, BS, DIN, ISO, UNI, DIN-EN
  • Customization is also available.

Carriage Bolts

A carriage bolt is a type of fastener with a round, domed head and a square section beneath the head to prevent turning. The shaft is fully threaded. Carriage bolts are commonly used in wood connections, where the square section grips the wood to keep the bolt from turning when tightened.


Technical Specification:

  • Diameter: M2 – M42
  • Length: 10mm to 150mm
  • Also available in different Materials & Grades
  • Standard: ISO 4017, DIN931, GB5783, DIN, ASTM, ANSI, EN, AS, JIS

T Bolts

A T-bolt is a fastener with a T-shaped head that fits into a slot or channel. It’s used to secure parts in place by sliding the head into the slot and then tightening with a nut. Common in machinery, construction, and automotive applications.


Technical Specification:

  • Size: M2 to M33
  • Length: 3 mm to 200 mm
  • Standard: IS, DIN, BS, ASTM, IS 4190, ISO, JIS, GB
  • Finish: Black Oxide, Zinc Plating, Hot Dip Galvanized
