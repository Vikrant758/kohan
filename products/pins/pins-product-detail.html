Clevis Pins

Clevis pins are cylindrical fasteners with a head at one end and one or more holes for securing with a cotter pin or wire. They are commonly used in agricultural machinery, construction equipment, and various industrial applications where quick assembly and disassembly are required. Clevis pins provide a reliable method for connecting and securing components in a robust yet easily removable manner.


Because of their straightforward design and ease of use, clevis pins are ideal for use in applications that require frequent assembly and disassembly, such as linkage mechanisms, pivot points, and other moving parts.


Technial Specifications

  • Standard & Specifications: ASME, DIN, ISO, JIS, GB.
  • Size Range: 1/8 inch to 1 inch in diameter, lengths from 1 inch to 6 inches
  • Material: Carbon steel, Stainless steel, Aluminum
  • Surface Finish: Zinc plating, Galvanization, Black oxide, Passivation
  • Tensile Strength: Varies based on material, with steel pins offering the highest strength
  • Design: Cylindrical body with a head at one end, one or more holes for securing with a cotter pin or wire

Dowel Pins

Dowel pins are cylindrical rods used primarily for precise alignment of parts in machinery, woodworking, and various industrial applications. They ensure that the components are correctly positioned and securely held in place. Dowel pins are available in various materials and are known for their high strength, precision, and reliability.


Due to their precision and reliability, dowel pins are ideal for applications that demand exact alignment and positioning, such as machinery assembly, tooling, and jigs.


Technial Specifications

  • Standard & Specifications: ASME, DIN, ISO, JIS, GB.
  • Size Range: 1/16 inch to over 1 inch in diameter, lengths from 1/4 inch to several inches
  • Material: Steel, Stainless steel, Hardened steel, Wood, Plastic
  • Surface Finish: Polished for low roughness average (RA), Zinc plating, Black oxide, Passivation
  • Hardness (for metal pins): Rockwell hardness rating: C36-58
  • Special Properties (for non-metallic pins): Flexibility, Corrosion resistance
