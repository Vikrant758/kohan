About Us

KOHAN INDUSTRIES PVT. LTD. is an ISO 9001:2015 accredited company and a proud member of the Kundan Group, a renowned name in the fasteners and CNC machined components manufacturing industry since 1978.

Located in the western suburban region of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, Kohan Industries Pvt. Ltd. stands as a beacon of excellence in the manufacturing sector. We specialize in producing high-quality, high-precision fasteners and CNC machined/turned components. Our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of quality management and continuous improvement is at the core of our operations.

Leadership

As a key entity within the prestigious Kundan Group of Companies, Kohan Industries Pvt. Ltd. has garnered an international reputation for its exceptional products and services. Our company is spearheaded by the visionary leadership of Mr. Uddharak Gupta, our Chairman & Managing Director, whose strategic direction and innovative mindset have been instrumental in propelling us to new heights.
