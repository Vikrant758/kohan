About Us

We are a leading manufacturer specializing in a wide range of high-quality fasteners, brass components, turned components, and sheet metal components. Our products are crafted from stainless steel and various specialized grades to meet diverse industrial needs. With an impressive in-house product design capability exceeding 150,000 unique designs, we pride ourselves on innovation and precision engineering.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities boast a total production capacity of 7,500 metric tons per annum. Additionally, we have ample land available for further expansion, ensuring we can meet increasing demands and continue to grow our production capabilities.

With a well-established presence in international markets, we export our products to over 50 countries worldwide. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in the global manufacturing industry.

Mission

Kohan wants to sustain profitable growth by providing the best customer experience through Quality & Reliability. With our success, we want to ensure that our Customers build their businesses & Employees build their futures.

Vision

Kohan wants to become the most successful and respected fastener manufacturing company in the Global Arena. Being the best means providing outstanding quality, service, and value, so that we make every customer satisfied in every way.
