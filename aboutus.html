About Us

An ISO 9001:2015 Accredited Company and a Proud Member of the Kundan Group.

Kohan Industries Pvt. Ltd. based in western sub-urban region of Mumbai. Maharashtra, INDIA; stands as a beacon of excellence in the manufacturing sector, specializing in high-quality, high precision fasteners and CNC machined/turned components. As an ISO 9001:2015 accredited company, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality management and continuous improvement.

Our Legacy and Leadership

Mr. Uddharak Gupta, our esteemed Managing Director, brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to Kohan Industries Pvt. Ltd.; Holding an MBA in Business Analytics from the prestigious SP Jain Institute of Management & Research (SPJIMR), Mumbai, Mr. Gupta's strategic direction and innovative mindset have been instrumental in propelling our company to new heights.


Under his visionary leadership, Kohan Industries has achieved significant milestones and garnered international repute. His deep understanding of business analytics and market dynamics has enabled us to implement cutting-edge solutions and optimize our operations. Mr. Gupta's commitment to excellence and continuous improvement is reflected in our unwavering focus on quality and customer satisfaction.


With a passion for innovation and a forward-thinking approach, Mr. Gupta has played a pivotal role in shaping the company's growth trajectory, ensuring that Kohan Industries remains at the forefront of the fasteners and CNC components manufacturing industry.

Uddharak Gupta, Managing Director

Our Expertise

At Kohan Industries, we pride ourselves on our technical expertise and state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities. Our advanced facilities are equipped with the latest High Speed Headers & Thread Rolling Machines & CNC machines set up, enabling us to produce precision components that meet the exacting standards of our global clientele. Our product range includes a diverse array of fasteners and custom CNC turned components, designed to cater to a wide spectrum of industrial applications.
